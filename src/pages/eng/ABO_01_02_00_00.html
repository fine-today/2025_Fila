History

Misto’s heritage, a journey of innovation and bold challenges.

history misto

2022~2025

  • 2025

    FILA Holdings Corp. changed its name to Misto Holdings Corp.

  • 2024

    FILA Holdings Corp. (now, Misto Holdings Corp.) was Included in the global ESG Index 'FTSE4Good' for the first time.

  • 2023

    Acquisition of H5LY NO7 Greater China trademark rights
    FILA Holdings was recognized as a Top-Rated Company in the 2023 ESG rankings by global ESG ratings agency Sustainalytics, becoming the first Korean fashion company in the Asia-Pacific textile and apparel industry to receive this distinction.

  • 2022

    Acquisition of Alife global trademark rights.

2018~2021

  • 2021

    Acquisition of Club Glove global trademark rights.

  • 2020

    Renamed to FILA Holdings Corp. and shifted to a holding company structure.

  • 2019

    Acquisition of KJUS global trademark rights.

  • 2018

    • FILA brand’s signature sneaker, the ‘Disruptor 2,’ won 'Shoe of the Year(FNAA)
      Acquisition of Links & Kings global trademark rights.

2007~2016

  • 2016

    Acushnet Holdings Corp. was listed on the New York Stock Exchange (Ticker: GOLF) and becomes a subsidiary of FILA Korea Ltd.

  • 2011

    Acquisition of the U.S. golf equipment company 'Acushnet Company'.

  • 2010

    Listed on the Korea Exchange (KOSPI: 081660).

  • 2007

    FILA Korea Ltd. acquired FILA global trademark rights.

1911~2005

  • 2005

    FILA Korea(now, Misto Holdings Corp.) became independent via Management Buyout from FILA Global.

  • 2000

    Titleist brand launched its very first Pro V1 golf ball.

  • 1991

    Establishment of FILA Korea Ltd.

  • 1973

    FILA brand introduced its first sportswear collection, marking its transition into a sportswear brand and unveiling the iconic ‘F-BOX’ logo.

  • 1911

    FILA brand was founded in Biella, Italy.
