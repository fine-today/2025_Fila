Benefit

Misto is always striving to improve the quality of life for our employees.

Misto Life

  • Welfare benefit

    Access a variety of lifestyle perks, including shopping, entertainment, and education.

  • Meals & Snacks

    Enjoy fresh daily meals at our in-house cafeteria and a variety of convenient snacks in the office.

  • In-house Cafe

    Relax with a full selection of coffee and beverages at discounted employee prices.

  • Exclusive product discounts

    Get special pricing on Misto brand products exclusively for employees.

  • Clubs & Communities

    Join employee-led clubs and connect with colleagues who share your interests.

  • Commuting support

    Misto helps offset the cost of your daily commute with transportation subsidies.

  • Mobile allowance

    To support seamless communication, we subsidize mobile phone expenses.

Health & Wellness

  • Medical examination

    We offer regular health checkups for employees and their families.

  • Lactation room & wellness space

    Dedicated spaces are available to support female employees’ well-being and nursing needs.

  • Extended Health Insurance

    Additional health benefits are offered to support employees and their families, covering a wide range of medical needs.

  • Wellness incentives

    We offer smoking cessation support and health benefit incentives for non-smokers.

Career Growth

  • Career support program

    We support employees in their ongoing learning through tuition reimbursement and external course sponsorship.

  • Career relevant certification support

    We support financial incentives to employees who obtain professional certifications, promoting personal and career growth.

  • Learning credits

    Employees receive support to purchase books and learning materials.

Family & Holidays

  • Life event support

    We offer thoughtful support during meaningful life moments such as weddings, bereavement, and family milestones.

  • Long-service recognition

    We honor employee's long-term dedication with recognition and appreciation for years of service.

  • Holiday gifts

    Seasonal gifts are shared in appreciation during major holidays.

  • New baby celebration

    We celebrate the birth of a new family member with a warm gesture of congratulations.

  • Birthday gift

    We deliver heartfelt congratulations along with a gift.

  • Welcome package

    New team members receive a welcome package to celebrate their first step at Misto.
