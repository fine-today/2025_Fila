Benefit

Misto is always striving to improve the quality of life for our employees.

Life

  • Welfare benefit

    Misto Holdings provides welfare benefit that employees can use according to their individual needs.

  • Meal support

    Misto Holdings provides delicious breakfast/lunch in the cafeteria, and offers snacks in the office.

  • In-house Cafe

    Misto Holdings has an in-house cafe that offers beverages and coffee at employee-discounted prices, providing a relaing break.

  • Employee discount

    Misto Holdings provides discounts on its brand products, offering employees a valuable purchasing benefit.

  • Employee clubs

    Misto Holdings supports employee clubs, helping employees share interests and come together.

  • Transportation support

    Misto Holdings provides transportation support to ease commuting expenses.

  • Mobile support

    Misto Holdings partially covers communication costs to ensure smooth work operations.

Health & Wellness

  • Employee health check-up program

    Misto Holdings provides regular health check-ups to ensure employees' well-being.

  • Female staff lounge

    Misto Holdings provides a comfortable lounge for female employees to relax.

  • Family health insurance

    Misto Holdings provides accident insurance to ensure the safety of employees and their families.

  • Support for non-smokers

    Misto Holdings encourages a non-smoking lifestyle, and partially provides partial health insurance support for non-smokers

Educational support

  • Educational support

    Misto Holdings offers educational programs to support employee growth.

  • Certification support

    Misto Holdings supports financial incentives to employees who obtain professional certifications, promoting personal and career growth.

  • Book purchase support

    Misto Holdings supports employees in purchasing books for learning and self-improvement.

Family & Holidays

  • Family event support

    Misto Holdings supports employees' life events such as marriage or condolences.

  • Long-service benefit

    Misto Holdings recognizes and rewards employees for their long-term dedication and service.

  • National holiday gift

    Misto Holdings expresses appreciation by providing gifts to employees during major holidays.

  • Newborn baby gift

    Misto Holdings celebrates the birth of employees' children with congratulatory gifts.

  • Birthday gift

    Misto Holdings delivers heartfelt congratulations along with a thoughtful gift

  • Welcome gift

    Misto Holdings offers a congratulatory gift to new employees joining the company as a welcome gesture.
